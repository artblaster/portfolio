MY BACKGROUND

I started drawing by copying characters off of video game boxes/manuals as a child. Then I studied art and game design in college. Lately I've been working freelance and continuing daily.

MY MEDIUM

I'm primarily a digital artist, I mostly work on a Wacom Cintiq and sometimes my iPad.
The software I normally use are Clip Studio Paint, Blender, and Aseprite.

MY INSPIRATION

I'm very influenced by early 3D videogames from the Playstation 1 and Nintendo 64 era, as well as game/movie concept art.
